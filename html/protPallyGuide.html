TBC Protection Paladin Tank Talents & Builds Guide - Burning Crusade Classic 2.5.1

Welcome to Wowhead's Paladin Tank Talents TBC Guide, updated for Phase 1 of Burning Crusade Classic! In this guide, we will cover every Paladin Talent in their talent tree, how useful it is in both PvE and PvP situations, as well as covering the best Paladin talent builds and best Paladin specs in both PvE and PvP environments for The Burning Crusade Classic. In this guide, we focus purely on The Burning Crusade endgame builds for PvE and PvP. If you're looking into talent tree builds that you can use while leveling your Paladin, check out our Paladin TBC Leveling Guide for more information. In The Burning Crusade Classic, Paladins must visit a Paladin Trainer in order to change their talent tree selections. This process has a cost attached to it, which starts as a small fee but increases every time you perform it, up to a cap of 50. This cost is reduced by 5 per month, to a minimum of 10. Our Paladin guides are always updated with the latest information from in-game experience, theorycrafting, and logs; make sure to check our changelog to this page, by clicking on View Changelog at the top of the page. Talent Builds for Proteciton Paladin Tank in Burning Crusade Deep Prot Tank Build 0/49/12 This is the recommended bread and butter dedicated tanking build for just about every dungeon and raid tanking scenario in TBC. This build's primary role in raids is to serve as dedicated raid tank, minimizing its effectiveness as a viable build for off-healing or DPS. For those who feel confident about not needing Avenger's Shield for pulling or for building a strong threat lead at the start of fights, this frees up an additional talent point that can be put into another talent such as Pursuit of Justice.
  • Righteous Defense—A taunt for Paladins! Can be cast on either allies or enemies, with a 40-yard range, making it a very powerful taunt.
  • Spiritual Attunement—A powerful passive mana regeneration ability! The more damage you take, the more healing you take, which means you will regenerate more mana. This ability shows its true potential in raids and heroic dungeons, allowing Paladin tanks to maintain their rotations near-indefinitely!
  • Avenger's Shield—A ranged ability that can hit up to three targets at once! Excellent for pulling mobs and bosses.
  • Avenging Wrath—An extremely powerful temporary buff that greatly boosts your threat generation, making it excellent for building threat leads at the start of encounters.
  • Seal of Corruption/ Seal of Vengeance—Seals that apply a stacking debuff which deal Damage over Time (DoT) as well as amplify the damage of Judgement. Although these seals are a bit RNG-dependent for maximizing threat, these seals have massive threat potential in various situations if used correctly.
  • Seal of Blood/ Seal of the Martyr—These seals deal a portion of your physical damage as holy damage. These seals are not necessarily powerful for Protection Paladins due to being more reliant on spellpower than physical damage for threat generation.
  • Crusader Aura—Provides additional mobility when riding mounts! Excellent for PvP or for flying around Outland!
